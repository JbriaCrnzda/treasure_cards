Keep these treasures in to your heart

God is love

John 3:16

He loves you with an everlasting love

There is a love that last forever, a love that is overwhelming in being faithful and good.

A love that you and I don't deserve, for we kept running away from Him.

But He is calling us and still cares for us, His love is chasing after us.

He found me and I surrender to him, will you also surrender to Him?

God is Hope

Jeremiah 29:11

He is my hope

His plans for you and me is hope and a future

This hope can't be taken away from us if we believe in him truthfully

His hope for you is better than everything and anything

I trust His hope for me, will you also put your trust in Him?
